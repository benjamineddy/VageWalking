Stratton

Bude

This is a longer walk than some that I post, however it is not overly strenuous due to its flat nature and has a nice mixture of coast, fields, sea and canal/river. If you are very lucky, you will be as blessed as we were, with a fine display by a rather large Kingfisher who seemed intent on leading us down the canal, plus several glimpses of Water Voles. We considered ourselves very lucky!

OS Explorer Map: 111
Start Ref: 205 067
End Ref: 205 067

Grading: Easy
Distance: 7.75 miles
Time: 2.5 - 3hrs
