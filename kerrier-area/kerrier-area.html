Kerrier

Carnmenellis

This is one of Cornwall`s five Marilyn`s. Carnmenellis has a highest point of 252m, and a prominence of 165m. This is a good circular walk around the outskirts of the hill (as the highest point is on private land). It is within touching distance of Stithians reservoir, so relatively centrally based.

OS Explorer Map: 104
Start Ref: 6885 3635
End Ref: 6885 3635

Grading: Easy
Distance: 3 miles
Time: 1.5hrs
