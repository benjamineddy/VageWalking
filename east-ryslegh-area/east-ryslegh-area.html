East Ryslegh

Cremyll

This pretty circular walk takes us along coastline and creeks, around country estates and protective forts from Napoleonic times. Not to mention pubs, cream teas and beaches. A superb day out in Cornwall.



OS Explorer Map: 108
Start Ref: 454 534
End Ref: 454 534

Grading: Easy
Distance: 6 miles

Kit Hill

Kit Hill This walk is set to take us around and over one of Cornwall`s five Marilyn`s. A Marilyn being a hill/mountain with a prominence over the surrounding area of at least 150m. Kit Hill has a prominence of 171m, although its height is 334m.It also has the honour of overlooking the site of one of the last major battles between the Cornish and the West Saxons.

OS Explorer Map: 108
Start Ref: 3835 7130
End Ref: 3835 7130

Grading: Easy - Medium
Distance: 7 miles
Time: 2.5rs

Rame Head

This circular walk takes us around Cornwall`s Forgotten Peninsular, and out to the beautiful Chapel on Rame Head itself. It also takes us past (and you can walk past this without seeing it) the Grotto at Penlee Point which is featured on the cover of our OS Explorer Map.



OS Explorer Map: 108
Start Ref: 431 503
End Ref: 431 503

Grading: Medium
Distance: 5 miles
